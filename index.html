About me

Hello and welcome, my name is Alexandru Georgian Ciobanu and this is the final project for my course at Code Nation showcasing the html/css knowledge i have gained during 1 week.

A little story about me, Before the Covid-19 i was working in hospitality as a head chef, but this didn't just come directly, I started as a kitchen porter, accumulated experience and I slowly became a Head Chef in my last 2 years. During the Covid-19 the limited company which I have worked for has been closed. The hospitality industry has been decimated during the pandemic, during this time, i decided to change my career into IT and learn new and exciting skills.

I have always been a fool for technology I have always loved new inventions, computers, gadgets and just about anything that involves computers and softwares. You may wonder why didn't I follow this career path earlier. Main reason is that my math and learning skills were very poor, and I was always been put off by this, I never really thought i could actually learn how to program or code, so i started playing around with softwares like photoshop, dreamweaver, illustrator and i have acquired skills needed to design food menus, kitchen signs, alter photos, and many other things which came in handy later on.

Things have changed since high school. Fast forward 10 years in the future i decided to try getting into tech again because of Covid-19 and Code Nation was there for me. They have introduced me to coding and software developing in a very exciting way.

I couldn't believe that i could code my first text based adventure game and a fully working atm cash machine software in Python programing language, a blog/website, all from codes in the Visual Studio Code. This not only has showed me that i can do things i never thought i could, but also made me review mathematics which i am slowly beginning to like.
